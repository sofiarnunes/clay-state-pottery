Clay State Pottery Gallery

a modern gallery for the city

Our gallery in Seatle is a dream-come-true for the pottery born in 2018. Jorah Smith, founder, and Rae Fiddle, potter, found each other and joined the concept of naturalism with elegance in a single peace of clay.

Their travels and discoveries of the evolution of time and life, gave them inspiration to create the collections you see here.

Contact us to book a tour towards our magical gallery and try to do your own peace.
